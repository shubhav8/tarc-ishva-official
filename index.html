About Developer

TARC is a leading luxury real estate developer in Delhi NCR, known for creating premium residences that blend timeless Indian heritage with contemporary living. Driven by a vision to redefine urban luxury, the brand focuses on architectural excellence, sustainability, wellness, and refined lifestyle experiences. With landmark developments across Delhi and Gurugram, TARC delivers thoughtfully designed homes featuring expansive spaces, elegant aesthetics, and world-class amenities. Inspired by India’s rich cultural essence, the developer integrates traditional values with modern innovation to create distinctive living environments. Backed by strong leadership and a commitment to quality, TARC continues to shape sophisticated residential experiences for modern homebuyers.
